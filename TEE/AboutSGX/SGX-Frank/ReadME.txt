- SGX folder is for paper that:
	1) Introduce SGX or SGX design
	2) Extensions on SGX either by Intel or reseachers to introduce additional functionalities
	
- SGX Security folder is for papers that:
	1) Research methods to attack SGX-based systems from:
		a) Side channels
		b) Architecture
	2) Research methods to quantify effectiveness of current security measures on SGX or patches to exploits
	
- SGX Application folder is for papers that:
	1) Research on applying SGX to different systems or libraries
	2) Usage of SGX along or in conjunction with other tools to speedup/reduce overhead of proposed idea
